# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** June 8, 2026
**Last updated:** June 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the **Entertain** mobile application
("Entertain", "the app", "we", "us", or "our") handles your information.

Entertain is a personal app for organising domestic events: it helps you
plan menus, manage ingredients, and prepare shopping lists for the
suppliers you work with.

## 2. What information we collect

Entertain is designed to be minimal. We only collect what is necessary
to make the app work.

### 2.1 Information you provide

You provide the following information by entering it into the app:

- **Event information**: event names, dates, types (seated, buffet,
  other), number of guests.
- **Catalog information**: dish names, descriptions, preparation notes,
  ingredients, quantities, units.
- **Supplier configuration**: supplier category labels, supplier names
  (if you choose to record them), phone numbers, email addresses,
  preferred messaging channel.
- **Message templates**: greeting and signature text for supplier
  messages.

This information is stored under an anonymous user account
automatically created on first launch. We do **not** ask for your
name, email address, phone number, address, age, or any other
personal identifier when you start using the app.

### 2.2 Information collected automatically

- **Anonymous user identifier**: when you first open the app, a random
  identifier is generated to associate your data with your device.
  This identifier is not linked to your name, email, or any other
  personally identifiable information.
- **Technical operational data**: basic technical information needed
  to keep the app functioning (database connection metadata, request
  timestamps). No analytics, tracking, advertising identifiers,
  device fingerprints, or location data are collected.

### 2.3 Information we do NOT collect

We do not collect:

- Your real name, postal address, or government-issued identifiers.
- Your phone contacts list.
- Your photos, files, or any content stored on your device outside
  the app.
- Your precise or approximate location.
- Browsing history, app usage analytics, or behavioural tracking.
- Advertising identifiers.
- Microphone or camera recordings.

## 3. Where your data is stored

Your data is stored on servers operated by **Supabase**
(https://supabase.com), located in **Frankfurt, Germany (European
Union)**. Supabase is a third-party infrastructure provider acting as
a data processor on behalf of the data controller.

All data transmission between the app and the storage servers is
encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS.

---

## 4. How we use your data

Your data is used solely for the operation of the app:

- To display your events, catalog, and shopping lists across app
  sessions.
- To compose supplier messages from your stored data when you choose
  to send them.
- To synchronise your data between any devices where you may install
  the app under the same anonymous user identifier.

We do **not** use your data for:

- Advertising or marketing.
- Profiling, analytics, or behavioural research.
- Selling, renting, or sharing with third parties for any commercial
  purpose.
- Training machine learning models.

---

## 5. Sharing with third parties

We do **not** share your data with any third party, except:

- **Supabase**, as the infrastructure provider that hosts the
  database. Supabase is contractually bound to handle your data only
  on our instructions and in compliance with the GDPR.
- **Legal obligations**: if required by a valid legal order (court
  order, law enforcement request) under applicable EU and Spanish law.

When you choose to **send a supplier message** through the app, the
content of that message is composed on your device and sent through
your chosen channel (WhatsApp, SMS, email, share sheet, etc.). At
that point, the message and its recipient are subject to the privacy
policies of the messaging service you use. We do not retain copies of
sent messages beyond the snapshot stored within the app for your own
reference.

## 6. Your rights under the GDPR

If you are located in the European Union, you have the following
rights under the General Data Protection Regulation:

- **Right of access**: to know what personal data we hold about you.
- **Right of rectification**: to correct inaccurate data.
- **Right of erasure** ("right to be forgotten"): to request the
  deletion of all your data.
- **Right to data portability**: to receive a copy of your data in a
  machine-readable format.
- **Right to object**: to object to the processing of your data.
- **Right to lodge a complaint**: with the Spanish Data Protection
  Agency (Agencia Española de Protección de Datos, www.aepd.es) or
  your local supervisory authority.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at
rafael.pous@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Because Entertain creates an anonymous account by default, we may
need additional information from you to identify your data when you
exercise these rights (for example, the anonymous user identifier
visible in the app's settings, if available).

Your data is retained as long as you continue to use the app. If you
request deletion, all your data will be permanently removed from our
storage within 30 days of the request.

We have no offline backup retention policy beyond the operational
backups maintained by Supabase for service continuity (typically
retained for a short period and used only for disaster recovery).
