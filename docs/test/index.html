Android beta · help me test it

Thanks for wanting to test Entertain — an app for people who love to host. It’s a closed test: a small group trying the app before it’s out properly.

Before you start

  • Android only for now — iPhone version on the way.
  • You’ll need the Google account you use on Google Play (a Gmail or Workspace address).
  • You’ll use the app over about 2 weeks of real use.
  • It’s free, with every feature open — including the AI ones.
  • Works in English, Catalan & Spanish, based on your phone’s language.
  • You’ll start with sample data to explore. When you’re ready, tap “Start fresh” to clear the examples and keep only what you’ve made.

1Join the testers group

This step isn’t optional — it’s how Google grants you access to the test. There are no discussions and no emails to read: joining the group is simply the switch that lets you install the app.

Join the testers group →

2Get your tester link

The moment you join, you’ll receive a welcome email from the group with your personal “Become a tester” link. Open it on your Android phone and tap the button.

Why the group first? The tester link only works once you’re a member — that’s why it’s sent by email after you join, rather than shown here.

3Install from Google Play

After becoming a tester, the same page gives you a “Download it on Google Play” link. Install it like any other app. (If Play shows an old version, give it a few minutes.)

How to send feedback

Inside the app: Settings → Suggestions. Tell me what worked, what didn’t, what confused you.

A small ask

Please stay in the test for the full 2 weeks and don’t leave the group until it’s over — it really helps me get it ready for everyone else.
