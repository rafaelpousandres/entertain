# Help me test Entertain 🍽️

Thanks for wanting to test **Entertain** — an app for people who love to host. It's a **closed test**: a small group trying the app before it's out properly.

## Before you start
- **Android only** for now (iPhone version on the way).
- You'll use the app **over about 2 weeks** of real use.
- It's **free**, with every feature open — including the AI ones.
- Works in **English, Catalan & Spanish**, automatically, based on your phone's language.
- **You'll start with sample data.** The app comes preloaded with example events, dishes and a shopping list so you can explore. Play freely — and when you're ready, tap **"Start fresh"** to clear the examples and keep only what you've created yourself.

## Step 1 — Join the testers group
Join with the Google account you use on Google Play:
👉 **[Join the testers group](https://groups.google.com/g/entertain-testers)**

## Step 2 — Become a tester
Once you've joined the group, open this link **on your Android phone** and tap **"Become a tester"**:
👉 **[OPT-IN LINK]**

## Step 3 — Install from Google Play
After opting in, the same page gives you a **"Download it on Google Play"** link. Install it like any other app. (If Play still shows an old version, give it a few minutes.)

## How to send feedback
Inside the app: **Settings → Suggestions**. Tell me what worked, what didn't, what confused you. Honest reactions are exactly what I'm after.

## A small ask
Please **stay in the test for the full 2 weeks** and don't leave the group until it's over — it really helps me get it ready for everyone else.
