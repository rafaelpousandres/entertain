# Entertain — Play Store descriptions (English) — Revised

This file collects the textual assets for the Google Play Store
listing. Three sections, each with the exact character count to fit
Play Store's limits.

This is the revised version after the title shortening discussion
and the broadening of the language to cover all gatherings (not
only dinners).

## Long description

Maximum: 4000 characters. Recommended length: 500–2000 characters
for high readability. This appears on the app's detail page.

```
Entertain is the calm way to organize the meals and gatherings you host at home.

Whether you're cooking for a Sunday brunch, a casual weeknight dinner with friends, or a Christmas Eve feast for ten, Entertain turns your event into a clear plan: menu, ingredients, and shopping organized by supplier — all in one place.


WHAT IT DOES

• Build your event's menu from your personal dish catalog.
• Adjust the number of servings for each dish — ingredient quantities scale automatically.
• See at a glance which ingredients you have at home, which you need to order, and which have arrived.
• Group your shopping list by supplier (your butcher, your fishmonger, the greengrocer, etc.).
• Send ready-to-go messages to your suppliers via WhatsApp or SMS — no copy-pasting.
• Keep a clean view of your events: in preparation, ready, and past.


WHO IT'S FOR

• Home cooks who like to host with thought and care.
• Couples and families who organize regular gatherings.
• Anyone who likes to entertain — from a quiet brunch to a holiday feast.


WHY IT'S DIFFERENT

Most recipe and meal-planning apps focus on cooking. Entertain focuses on the event: the people coming, the menu you'll serve them, and the choreography of getting everything ready on time. It's the missing layer between your favorite recipes and a successful gathering.


KEY FEATURES

• Multilingual: Catalan, Spanish, English.
• Privacy-first: anonymous accounts by default, no tracking, no ads, your data stays in the EU.
• Photos: add photos to dishes, ingredients, and events to remember what worked.
• Reusable: duplicate past events as a starting point for new ones.
• Free to use.


GET STARTED

Open the app and start adding the dishes you cook often. Each dish has its ingredients with quantities — the app handles scaling when you build a menu. Configure your usual suppliers once, and from then on, every event you organize composes its own shopping list automatically.

No account is needed to start using Entertain. All your data is private to you.


CONTACT

For questions, feedback, or privacy inquiries: rafael.pous@gmail.com

Privacy policy: https://rafaelpousandres.github.io/entertain/privacy/
```

(2,210 characters — well within the 4000 limit.)
